Chicken Salad

Ingredients:

Lettuce, Chicken, Dressing

Instructions:

Dice chicken and chop lettuce. Mix together in a large bowl and add in dressing. Stir until combined.

Fruit Smoothie

Ingredients:

Frozen Blueberries, Banana, Greek Yogurt, Orange Juice

Instructions:

Slice banana and place all ingredients in blender and blend until smooth.

Mashed Potatoes

Ingredients:

Potatoes, Sour cream, Butter

Instructions:

Dice potatoes and add to pot with water to cover. Boil until tender, drain water and add sour cream and butter, mash until smooth.

Turkey Sandwich

Ingredients:

Turkey, Bread, Lettuce, Pickles

Instructions:

Place turkey flat on bottom side of bread. Then place lettuce and pickles, and any desired condiments.

Shrimp Tacos

Ingredients:

Shrimp, Cabbage, Mayomnaise, Tortillas

Instructions:

Combine cabbage and mayonaise. Place shrimp inside of tortillas, top with cabbage mix, and desired condiments.

Pepperoni Pizza

Ingredients:

Dough, Sauce, Cheese, Pepperoni

Instructions:

Roll dough into a large circle, fill with sauce and top with cheese and pepperoni. Bake until dough is cooked through and cheese is melted.
